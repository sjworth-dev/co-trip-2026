Print + bind

1. Print on cardstock — 9 pages, single-sided, color, no margins.

2. Stack in order (cover on top).

3. Bind: hole-punch + binder rings (durable for a baby), or take to FedEx Office / Staples for $10 saddle-stitch.

4. Optional: laminate each page first for chew-proof board-book feel.

In your browser's print dialog: enable "Background graphics" so black pages print black.

For Jonah

A book about your first trip
Seven places · May–June 2026
Base 01Colorado Springs
A hawk!

She landed on Daddy's glove. Her wings were wide. Her eyes were sharp.

Jonah looked, and looked, and looked.

Base 02Salida
The river.

Whoosh, whoosh — the water ran cold and fast right through the middle of town.

Jonah watched the boats go by.

Base 03Pagosa Springs
Splash!

The hot springs were warm and steamy. Mommy dipped Jonah's little toes in the water.

Jonah smiled.

Base 04Durango
Choo-choo.

The big black train rolled slowly down the track, puffing white smoke into the tall pine trees.

Jonah heard the whistle blow.

Base 05Ridgway & Ouray
Mountains.

Tall, tall mountains stood all around. Snow on top. Green below. Big blue sky above.

Jonah looked up, and up, and up.

Base 06Redstone
A little cabin.

Smoke curled from the chimney. The stars came out, one by one. The hot tub bubbled warm.

Jonah was cozy and close to Mommy.

Base 07Carbondale
Round and round.

Daddy's bike spun up the empty mountain road. The sky turned pink behind the peaks.

Jonah waited for Daddy to come home.

The end
Goodnight, Jonah.

The moon came up over the mountains. The trip was over. You were small, and we held you a lot.

Goodnight, little traveler. We love you.
